    This website is for searching animes. You can type in name of animes and you can find more about it! You can also find out about similar animes. Please go to the anime section to search for animes. Enjoy and have fun searching for your favourite animes! Take note that anime titles must be searched in japanese (for example Your Lie in April written as Shigatsu wa Kimi no Uso or Kimiuso ) There is also a list for you to add in animes that you might be interested in.
